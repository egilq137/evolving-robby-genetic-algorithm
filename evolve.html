What is this?

Robby lives on a 10×10 grid littered with cans. He sees only his own cell and its four neighbours, and has no memory. His whole brain is a table of 243 rules — one action for each thing he might see. A genetic algorithm breeds these tables over generations, keeping what scores well, until Robby cleans up almost perfectly.

How to use it

  1. Press Play evolution (or +100 gens) and watch best fitness climb past M.
  2. Pause any time; tweak the presets and hit New run to compare.
  3. Below, pick Champion and step through a session to see how the evolved robot behaves.

The strategies

  • Champion / Best-ever — the evolved brains (Champion refreshes as evolution runs).
  • Manual (M) — Mitchell's hand-written rule; scores ~346.
  • Random walk / Random strategy — baselines that wander or self-trap.
