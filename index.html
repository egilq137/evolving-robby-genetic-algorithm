One sampled 10×10 grid

Filled circles are cans, blank cells are empty. Each cell gets a can with probability 0.5, independently.

Per-cell can frequency over 5000 grids

Each cell colored by how often it held a can across 5000 fresh layouts. Uniformly near 50% everywhere = the probability is per-cell, not a fluke.

Sensing — click a cell to place Robby

Robby (blue) senses only his 5 outlined cells. Click the four corners: each should report a WALL on exactly two sides. Click a middle cell: no walls at all.

Action stepper — apply one action, watch the score

Click an action to apply it to Robby (blue). Each step shows its reward (+10 pick up a can, −1 pick up nothing, −5 crash into a wall, 0 move/stay) and the running total. Undo reverts the last step so you can compare the score before and after.

computing…

Fitness = average session score over 100 random grids. Every fixed and random strategy scores badly — the maximum possible is ≈ 500. This is the baseline the genetic algorithm will have to improve on.
